Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

How do I create a resume with ATC Resume?

Sign up, log in, and go to the "Build Resume" page. Fill out the form with your details, preview your resume, and download it as a PDF. It’s designed to be ATS-friendly and easy to use.

Is ATC Resume free to use?

Yes, our basic resume-building features are free. You can create, preview, and download a professional resume without any cost.

What if I can’t download my PDF?

Ensure all required fields are filled. If the issue persists, try another browser or contact support at support@atcresume.com.

Can I edit my resume after downloading?

Yes, as long as you’re logged in, your data is saved. Return to the "Build Resume" page to edit and generate a new PDF.

How do I make my resume ATS-friendly?

Use keywords from the job description, avoid complex formatting, and stick to standard sections like Education and Experience. Our tool automatically optimizes for ATS.

What if I forget my password?

A "Forgot Password" feature is coming soon. For now, email support@atcresume.com to reset your account.

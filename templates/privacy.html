Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 03-07-2025

ATC Resume Builder ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. DATA COLLECTION & USAGE

We are designed to be privacy-first. We do NOT store your personal resume data on our servers.

  • Session Data: When you use the Resume Builder, the information you enter (name, email, skills, etc.) is stored temporarily in your browser's session. This data is required to generate your resume preview and PDF. This session is automatically deleted when you close your browser.
  • Uploaded Resumes: When you use the Resume Analyzer or other AI tools, your uploaded PDF is processed on our server to extract the text. This text is sent to the Google Gemini API for analysis and then immediately discarded from our system. The file itself is not stored after processing.
  • Newsletter: If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is strictly optional and requires your explicit consent.

2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES (GOOGLE GEMINI API)

To provide our AI features, we send the text content from your resume (or summary prompts) to the Google Gemini API. We do not send any personally identifiable file names. Google's use of this data is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

3. COOKIES

We use a necessary cookie for security purposes (CSRF token) to protect our forms. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

4. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

As we do not store your personal data, the rights of access, rectification, and erasure are fulfilled by our system's design. You have full control over the data you input, and it is removed when your session ends.

5. SECURITY

We implement security measures like CSRF protection and best practices to protect the data during its brief processing period. All communication with our server is encrypted via HTTPS.

6. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.

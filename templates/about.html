About ATC Resume Builder

ATC Resume Builder is an AI-powered platform dedicated to empowering job seekers. Our mission is to level the playing field in the competitive job market by providing cutting-edge tools to create ATS-friendly resumes, generate cover letters, match jobs, and analyze skill gaps.

Built by Prajwal A Bhandagi, our platform combines advanced AI technology with a user-friendly interface to help you achieve your career goals. Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, our tools are designed to help you stand out.

Our Mission
